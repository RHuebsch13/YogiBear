Providing yoga classes for Adults, Children, Teens & Families.

Classes based in Llandaff North, Cardiff, CF14

Why Yoga?

Through yoga practice you will learn to focus, concentrate & enhance your patience as well as build strength, coordination and improve flexibility

Wellbeing and Self Care

When a person is regularly encouraged to seek calm & be mindful they are more capable of concentrating enabling them to further their potential.

Keeping Active & Healthy

Our yoga practice encourages people to be active, find calm, interact in a non competitive class.

Giving You Space to Grow

Emotional wellbeing & mindfulness are our core. Our classes will encourage everyone to seek calm via a combination of yoga asanas & breathing exercises.
