Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa yoga can often be considered the most athletic style of yoga. In our vinyasa classes, we try to coordinated movement with your breath to flow from one pose to another. The focus is alignment-based and each class included new choreograph flows. You will be building strength and flexibility during these classes.

Yin Yoga

Yin yoga is a slow-paced style of yoga. There are many seated postures that are held for longer periods of time. Our Yin classes are great for beginners, they are also very popular for stretching your connective tissue and reducing stress and anxiety. The classes are relaxed, as you're supposed to let gravity do most of the work.

Hot Yoga

Hot yoga is our most intense workout. The studio in heated and the flow combine a vinyasa flow with quick cardio bursts. It is believed that hot yoga helps release toxins due to the heat and humidity in the studio during the flow.

Prenatal Yoga

Our prenatal yoga is carefully adapted for moms-to-be. Women in all trimesters are welcome. These classes are very good exercise for expectant moms because of the pelvic floor work, focus on breathing, and bonding with baby. It also helps mothers prepare for labor and delivery.
